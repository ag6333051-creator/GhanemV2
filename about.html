About

Independent designer,
quietly obsessive.

Eight years designing across print, screen and motion — currently based in Lisbon, working worldwide.

I'm Ahmed Ezat — a graphic designer, video editor and visual artist. My work sits at the intersection of editorial precision, cinematic pace, and playful, sometimes surreal, visual language.

I partner with founders, in-house teams and agencies who care about craft. Every engagement is small, senior and deeply hands-on — no juniors, no handoffs, no diluted decisions.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me shooting 35mm, digging through record crates, or scoring bad TV shows just to see if the edit can be saved.
